# Sainsbury's Store Layout

Sainsbury's stores typically have produce and bakery near the entrance.

Organize items into these areas, ordered by typical Sainsbury's layout:

1. **produce** (area_order: 1) - Fresh fruit, vegetables, salads
2. **bakery** (area_order: 2) - Fresh bread, in-store bakery
3. **dairy** (area_order: 3) - Milk, eggs, cheese, yogurt
4. **meat** (area_order: 4) - Fresh meat, fish, poultry
5. **deli** (area_order: 5) - Counter service, olives, antipasti
6. **frozen** (area_order: 6) - Frozen foods
7. **pantry** (area_order: 7) - Tinned goods, pasta, rice
8. **world_foods** (area_order: 8) - International cuisine
9. **breakfast** (area_order: 9) - Cereals, porridge
10. **snacks** (area_order: 10) - Crisps, biscuits
11. **confectionery** (area_order: 11) - Chocolate, sweets
12. **drinks** (area_order: 12) - Soft drinks, water, juice
13. **tea_coffee** (area_order: 13) - Hot drinks
14. **alcohol** (area_order: 14) - Wine, beer, spirits
15. **household** (area_order: 15) - Cleaning products
16. **health_beauty** (area_order: 16) - Toiletries, pharmacy
17. **baby** (area_order: 17) - Baby supplies
18. **pet** (area_order: 18) - Pet food and supplies
19. **other** (area_order: 19) - Miscellaneous

Taste the Difference and Sainsbury's Basics items should be categorized by product type.
