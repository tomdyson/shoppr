# Waitrose Store Layout

Waitrose typically has a premium layout with produce and bakery near the entrance.

Organize items into these areas, ordered by typical Waitrose layout:

1. **produce** (area_order: 1) - Fresh fruits, vegetables, salads
2. **bakery** (area_order: 2) - In-store bakery, artisan breads
3. **dairy** (area_order: 3) - Milk, cheese, eggs, yogurt, butter
4. **meat** (area_order: 4) - Butcher counter, packaged meat, fish
5. **deli** (area_order: 5) - Deli counter, olives, antipasti, cheese counter
6. **pantry** (area_order: 6) - Canned goods, pasta, oils, vinegars
7. **world_foods** (area_order: 7) - International foods
8. **breakfast** (area_order: 8) - Cereals, spreads, honey
9. **snacks** (area_order: 9) - Crisps, nuts, biscuits
10. **confectionery** (area_order: 10) - Chocolate, sweets
11. **drinks** (area_order: 11) - Soft drinks, juice
12. **tea_coffee** (area_order: 12) - Tea, coffee (premium selection)
13. **alcohol** (area_order: 13) - Wine, beer, spirits (extensive wine section)
14. **frozen** (area_order: 14) - Frozen foods
15. **household** (area_order: 15) - Cleaning, eco-friendly products
16. **health_beauty** (area_order: 16) - Toiletries, pharmacy
17. **baby** (area_order: 17) - Baby products
18. **pet** (area_order: 18) - Pet food
19. **other** (area_order: 19) - Miscellaneous

Waitrose Essential and own-brand items should be categorized by product type.
