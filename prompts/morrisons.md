# Morrisons Store Layout

Morrisons is known for its Market Street concept with fresh counters.

Organize items into these areas, ordered by typical Morrisons layout:

1. **produce** (area_order: 1) - Fresh fruits, vegetables
2. **bakery** (area_order: 2) - In-store bakery (Market Street)
3. **deli** (area_order: 3) - Deli counter, pie shop, fishmonger (Market Street)
4. **meat** (area_order: 4) - Butcher counter, packaged meat
5. **dairy** (area_order: 5) - Milk, cheese, eggs, yogurt
6. **pantry** (area_order: 6) - Canned goods, pasta, rice
7. **breakfast** (area_order: 7) - Cereals, spreads
8. **snacks** (area_order: 8) - Crisps, nuts, biscuits
9. **confectionery** (area_order: 9) - Chocolate, sweets
10. **drinks** (area_order: 10) - Soft drinks, juice
11. **tea_coffee** (area_order: 11) - Tea, coffee
12. **alcohol** (area_order: 12) - Beer, wine, spirits
13. **frozen** (area_order: 13) - Frozen foods
14. **household** (area_order: 14) - Cleaning products
15. **health_beauty** (area_order: 15) - Toiletries, pharmacy
16. **baby** (area_order: 16) - Baby products
17. **pet** (area_order: 17) - Pet supplies
18. **other** (area_order: 18) - Miscellaneous

Market Street counters (bakery, deli, butcher, fishmonger) are usually near the entrance.
