# Generic Supermarket Layout

Organize items into these areas, ordered by typical UK supermarket layout:

1. **produce** (area_order: 1) - Fresh fruits, vegetables, salads, fresh herbs
2. **bakery** (area_order: 2) - Bread, rolls, pastries, cakes, croissants
3. **dairy** (area_order: 3) - Milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, cream, eggs
4. **meat** (area_order: 4) - Fresh meat, poultry, fish, bacon, sausages
5. **deli** (area_order: 5) - Deli counter items, cooked meats, olives, antipasti
6. **frozen** (area_order: 6) - Frozen meals, ice cream, frozen vegetables, frozen pizza
7. **pantry** (area_order: 7) - Canned goods, pasta, rice, sauces, condiments, oils, vinegar
8. **breakfast** (area_order: 8) - Cereals, porridge, spreads, jam, honey
9. **snacks** (area_order: 9) - Crisps, nuts, biscuits, crackers
10. **confectionery** (area_order: 10) - Chocolate, sweets, candy
11. **drinks** (area_order: 11) - Soft drinks, juice, water, squash
12. **tea_coffee** (area_order: 12) - Tea, coffee, hot chocolate
13. **alcohol** (area_order: 13) - Beer, wine, spirits, mixers
14. **household** (area_order: 14) - Cleaning products, paper goods, laundry, bin bags
15. **health_beauty** (area_order: 15) - Toiletries, medicine, shampoo, soap, toothpaste
16. **baby** (area_order: 16) - Baby food, nappies, baby care
17. **pet** (area_order: 17) - Pet food, pet supplies
18. **other** (area_order: 18) - Anything that doesn't fit above

## Guidelines

- If an item could fit multiple categories, choose the most specific one
- "Milk" goes in dairy, not drinks
- "Orange juice" goes in drinks, not produce
- "Frozen peas" goes in frozen, not produce
- When in doubt, use "other"
