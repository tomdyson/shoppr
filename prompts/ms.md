# M&S Food Hall Layout

M&S Food Halls focus on premium ready meals and quality fresh produce.

Organize items into these areas, ordered by typical M&S layout:

1. **produce** (area_order: 1) - Fresh fruits, vegetables, salads
2. **bakery** (area_order: 2) - In-store bakery, bread, pastries
3. **dairy** (area_order: 3) - Milk, cheese, eggs, yogurt
4. **meat** (area_order: 4) - Fresh meat, fish, poultry
5. **deli** (area_order: 5) - Prepared foods, ready meals, sandwiches
6. **pantry** (area_order: 6) - Canned goods, pasta, sauces
7. **breakfast** (area_order: 7) - Cereals, spreads
8. **snacks** (area_order: 8) - Crisps, nuts, biscuits
9. **confectionery** (area_order: 9) - Chocolate, sweets, Percy Pigs
10. **drinks** (area_order: 10) - Soft drinks, juice
11. **tea_coffee** (area_order: 11) - Tea, coffee
12. **alcohol** (area_order: 12) - Wine, beer, spirits
13. **frozen** (area_order: 13) - Frozen foods, frozen ready meals
14. **household** (area_order: 14) - Limited cleaning products
15. **health_beauty** (area_order: 15) - Toiletries
16. **other** (area_order: 16) - Miscellaneous

Note: M&S Food Halls vary in size. Larger stores have more fresh counters.
Ready meals and prepared foods are a key M&S specialty - often near deli section.
